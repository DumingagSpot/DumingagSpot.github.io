  Dumingag gained more attention in the past years, and there are two main contributors that I could think of. First, the passion and dedication of Dumingag Tourism staffs to promote the municipality and second, the articles and feedback posted online by varied satisfied costumers who have been enchanted by the municipality’s beauty.

   On July 29 & 30 Dumingag lunch night market opening. Vendors enthusiastically displayed their goods and products; because of their exquisite delicious foods and best quality products, vendors started to gain regular costumers.

   Dumingag night market isn’t just about whole lots of eating but it was filled with colored lights, boisterous crowds, some shopping, jamming with different bands and events you wouldn’t think of. Indeed! Dumingag night market is a “must-go, must-see, must-eat and must-fun” stop on.
